Created: 7/17/2020

OOF Difficult Obby, formely named OOFandLOLBOM's Difficult Obby, was created in July 2020, and is currently our most popular game.
The objective is to complete a huge obstacle course, that includes over 100 stages.
The uniqueness of it is that the difficulty progressively increases as the player completes each stage.
